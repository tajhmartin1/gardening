My Zinnias

Background

The zinnia elegans, or most notably known as the common zinnia, is one of the hardiest plants. Zinnias are a great starter flower because they are low maintenance and fast-growing. They are native to the Southwestern United States to South America. Zinnias come in a wide variety of colors and shapes making them perfect for everyone!

Care Needs

Zinnias prefer bright light and constant moisture for full blooms. They also benefit from fertilizer every 3-4 weeks to encourage growth. After the zinnias flower cut off the flower to allow for more growth.
