My Gomphrena

Background

Gomphrenas old-fashioned cottage garden favorite. Gomphrenas are annuals meaning that they die when the first frost hits and they leave their seeds behind. They are fast-growing tall plants (around 4ft tall).

Care Needs

These are some of the easiest plants to care for. It tolerates light shade, drought, and heat. However, they thrive under direct sunlight. Since they prefer a lot of light that means they prefer lots o water. Generally, you should water your gomphrena deeply once a week and water lightly when the topsoil is dry.
